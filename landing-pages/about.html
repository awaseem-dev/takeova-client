Our mission

We build the whole business — not just the website.

TAKEOVA exists for the millions of small-business owners who were told to "just get a website," then left to wire together a dozen tools they never asked to learn. We think the software should do that part.

Running a small business shouldn't require a tech team.

A plumber, a yoga teacher, a café owner — they're experts at their craft, not at stitching together a website builder, a booking app, a CRM, an invoicing tool, an email platform and a payment processor that all refuse to talk to each other.

So most owners either pay a developer they can't really afford, or duct-tape free tools together and lose hours every week to admin. Neither is good enough.

You describe it. TAKEOVA builds it — then runs it.

Tell TAKEOVA what you do and it generates your website: design, copy, layout, structure. But the site is just the front door. Behind it, TAKEOVA sets up the whole operation — taking bookings, tracking customers, sending invoices, collecting payments, following up by email.

Then it staffs that operation with AI employees who handle the repetitive work, so the business keeps moving whether or not you're at a screen.
