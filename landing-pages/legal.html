Legal & Privacy

On this page

Information We Collect

When you create a TAKEOVA account we collect the following categories of data:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure bcrypt hash, never plain text)
  • Site content — websites, pages, products, courses, and any content you create inside TAKEOVA
  • Payment information — billing details processed entirely by Stripe. TAKEOVA never stores card numbers, CVCs, or bank details
  • Usage data — which features you use, how often, and how your sites perform
  • Contact data — customers and leads you import or who submit forms on your sites
  • Communications — support tickets and emails you send to our team

✅ We never sell your data. We never have and never will.

⚙️
How We Use Your Data
  • To provide, operate, and improve the TAKEOVA platform
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send transactional emails — order confirmations, password resets, billing receipts
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests
  • To send product updates and announcements (opt out any time)
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • To comply with legal obligations
🗄️
Data Storage & Security
  • All data stored on encrypted servers (AES-256 at rest)
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt — impossible to reverse
  • All API traffic encrypted via HTTPS / TLS 1.3
  • Session tokens (JWT) expire automatically and are invalidated on logout
  • Database backups taken daily and stored in a separate region

Security Overview

Security is built into every layer of the TAKEOVA platform — from how we store passwords to how we process payments.

🔒 TLS 1.3🔑 AES-256💳 PCI DSS via Stripe🛡️ bcrypt🏥 Daily backups
🏗️
Infrastructure Security
  • Encryption in transit — all traffic uses TLS 1.3. HTTP automatically redirected to HTTPS
  • Encryption at rest — all databases and file storage encrypted with AES-256
  • DDoS protection — rate limiting and IP-level protection on all API endpoints
  • Access controls — TAKEOVA staff access to production is logged, audited, and restricted to need-to-know
  • Dependency scanning — automated vulnerability scanning of all packages on every deploy
👤
Account Security
  • Password hashing — bcrypt with cost factor 12 and salt. We cannot see or recover your password
  • Session management — JWT tokens expire automatically and are invalidated on logout
  • Brute force protection — accounts locked after repeated failed login attempts
  • Mobile app — auth tokens stored in the device's secure enclave (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
  • Biometric authentication — optional Face ID / Touch ID in the mobile app
💳
Payment Security

TAKEOVA never touches card data. All payment processing is handled by Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1 — the highest certification level).

  • Card numbers, CVCs, and expiry dates never pass through TAKEOVA servers
  • Stripe tokenises all card data before it leaves the customer's browser
  • Your Stripe Connect account is separate from TAKEOVA — you control your payouts directly
🏥
Backups & Recovery
  • Automated database backups every 24 hours
  • Backups stored in a geographically separate region
  • Backups retained for 30 days
  • Recovery Point Objective (RPO): 24 hours · Recovery Time Objective (RTO): 4 hours

⚠️ Please do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities before we've had a chance to fix them.
