Getting Started
Account setup, site creation, Stripe connection, and your first sale
1
🔑
Create your TAKEOVA account & set up your workspace
Free · All plans
▼
1
Sign up at takeova.ai
Click Get Started Free. Enter your name, email, and a password. You'll receive a verification email — click the link inside to confirm your account.
2
Complete your profile
Add your business name, timezone, and upload a profile photo. This information appears in your public profile and in emails sent to customers.
3
Choose your plan
Start with the free 3-day trial of any plan. You won't be charged until the trial ends. You can downgrade or cancel any time from Settings → Billing.
2
🎨
Build your site with the AI Site Builder
Starter+
▼
1
Go to Sites → New Site → Build with AI
Describe your business in 1–3 sentences (e.g. "I'm a personal trainer offering online coaching and workout plans"). The AI generates a complete homepage in seconds.
2
Customise your colours, fonts, and logo
Open the Style panel on the right. Change your primary colour, font pairing, and button style. Upload your logo (PNG or SVG, transparent background recommended).
3
Edit each section in the visual editor
Click any text to edit it inline. Drag sections to reorder. Add new sections (hero, features, pricing, testimonials, FAQ, contact) from the + panel.
4
Publish your site
Click Publish. Your site is live at yourname.takeova.ai immediately. Connect a custom domain from Settings → Custom Domain (Growth+ plans).
3
💳
Connect Stripe and accept your first payment
Starter+
▼
1
Go to Settings → Payments → Connect Stripe
Click Connect with Stripe. You'll be redirected to Stripe to create or log into your Stripe account. Stripe handles all payment processing — TAKEOVA never stores card data.
2
Complete Stripe onboarding
Stripe will ask for your business details, bank account, and ID verification. This typically takes 5–10 minutes and is required to receive payouts.
3
Test with a $1 product
Create a test product for $1. Use Stripe's test card number 4242 4242 4242 4242 to simulate a successful purchase and confirm the flow works end-to-end.
ℹ️TAKEOVA charges a tiered platform fee on each transaction (2.5% on Starter, 2.0% on Growth, 1.5% on Pro, 1.0% on Enterprise). Stripe also charges their standard processing fee (~2.9% + 30¢).
4
🌐
Connect a custom domain
Growth+
▼
1
Go to Sites → Your Site → Settings → Custom Domain
Enter your domain (e.g. yourbusiness.com). TAKEOVA will show you the DNS records you need to add.
2
Add a CNAME record at your domain registrar
Log in to your registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, etc.). Add a CNAME record pointing your domain to takeova.ai. For root domains (@), use an ALIAS or ANAME record instead.
3
Wait for DNS propagation and verify
DNS changes can take 5 minutes to 48 hours. Click Verify in TAKEOVA. Once verified, SSL is provisioned automatically — your site will be live at your custom domain with HTTPS.
5
📧
Set up your email sending domain
Starter+
▼
1
Go to Settings → Email → Sending Domain
Enter your domain. TAKEOVA generates SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records for you to add to your DNS.
2
Add the three DNS records
Add all three TXT records at your domain registrar. These prove ownership and dramatically improve email deliverability.
3
Click Verify and send a test
Once verified, send a test email to yourself. Your emails will now come from [email protected] instead of a shared @takeova.ai address.
💡Sending from your own domain significantly improves open rates and reduces the chance of your emails landing in spam.

AI Receptionist
A real phone number answered 24/7 by your AI — captures leads, answers questions, takes booking requests. Add-on: $99/mo, includes 100 mins/mo
1
📱
Get your AI phone number
Add-on: $99/mo
▼
1
Activate AI Receptionist and pick a number
Go to AI Employees → toggle AI Receptionist on. Open the Voice Agent tab → Get a Number. No Twilio account needed — TAKEOVA provisions the number for you from our own infrastructure. Choose your country, and optionally filter by area code or a number pattern (e.g. ends in 0000).
2
Pick a number from the list and click Provision
TAKEOVA purchases the number, wires up the AI receptionist webhook automatically, and assigns it to your account. The whole process takes about 5 seconds. You'll see your new number immediately.
3
Put the number everywhere
Add it to your website header, Google Business Profile, email signature, and social profiles. Anyone who calls will be answered by your AI receptionist — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
ℹ️The AI Receptionist is a $99/mo add-on available on any TAKEOVA plan. Includes 100 mins/mo. Minutes beyond that are metered at tiered rates starting at $0.15/min. Fresh minutes are credited automatically each billing cycle.
2
🎙️
Configure your AI receptionist
Add-on
▼
1
Go to Voice Agent → Settings
Set your custom greeting (e.g. "Thanks for calling Sarah's Coaching — how can I help you today?"). If you leave it blank, the AI uses your business name from your site.
2
Add business context and rules
The AI already knows your products, services, pricing, bookable services, and upcoming events from your TAKEOVA account. Add any extra rules here: e.g. "Never discuss competitor pricing", "Always offer a free consultation for enquiries over $500", "Our office is closed on Sundays".
3
What the AI handles automatically
The AI answers product and pricing questions, quotes service prices, takes booking requests (capturing name, date preference, and contact details), detects buyer intent, and adds every caller to your CRM. If the caller asks to speak to a person, it notes it in the transcript and flags the call for your follow-up.
💡The AI caps calls at 20 conversation turns to prevent runaway costs. For unusually long enquiries, it politely wraps up and suggests the caller visit your website.
3
👥
Review calls, leads, and transcripts
Add-on
▼
1
Go to Voice Agent → Call Log
Every call is logged with the caller's number, time, duration, and full transcript. Click any call to read the complete conversation turn by turn.
2
Leads are captured automatically
When a caller mentions wanting to book, buy, or provides their email during the call, TAKEOVA automatically creates a contact in your CRM tagged phone-lead. Their name and email are extracted from natural speech (e.g. "my email is sarah at gmail dot com").
3
Check your Voice AI stats
The stats panel shows total calls handled, calls today, leads captured, and average conversation length. Use this to gauge how busy your AI receptionist is and whether your voiceMinutes plan is sufficient.
4
📞
Make outbound AI calls
Add-on
▼
1
Go to Voice Agent → Outbound → New Call
Select a contact from your CRM and write a short script or purpose (e.g. "Appointment reminder for tomorrow at 2pm" or "Follow up on unpaid invoice INV-0042"). The AI calls them on your behalf from your provisioned number.
2
The call is fully conversational
The AI delivers the opening message then stays on the line to handle responses — if the contact asks a question, changes their appointment, or wants to call back, the AI handles it and logs the outcome in their CRM record.
⚠️Only call contacts who have consented to receive calls from you. Outbound AI calls must comply with local telemarketing laws (TCPA in the US, PECR in the UK).

1
⚡
Set up an outbound webhook
Growth+
▼
1
Go to Settings → Webhooks → Add Webhook
Enter the destination URL (your server endpoint or a service like Make / n8n / Pipedream). Choose which events to send: New Order, Form Submitted, New Contact, Booking Confirmed, Invoice Paid, or New Member.
2
Set a signing secret
Add a webhook secret. TAKEOVA includes a signature in every request header — verify this on your server to confirm the payload is genuinely from TAKEOVA and not a spoofed request.
3
Test and monitor deliveries
Click Send Test to fire a test payload. Go to Webhook Logs to see every delivery attempt, response code, and response body — makes debugging easy.
2
🔑
Use the TAKEOVA REST API
Growth+
▼
1
Get your API key from Settings → Integrations → API
Your API key is a bearer token. Include it in every request: Authorization: Bearer YOUR_KEY. Keep it secret — treat it like a password.
2
Available read endpoints
GET /api/appstore/api/contacts, /api/orders, /api/products, /api/invoices, /api/bookings. All return JSON with pagination support.
3
Available write endpoints
POST /api/contacts to create contacts programmatically. POST /api/orders/:id/fulfill to mark an order as fulfilled from an external system.
ℹ️For no-code automation, Zapier and Make are easier than the raw API. Use the raw API when you need custom server-side logic.
