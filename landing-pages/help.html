Common questions

Can't find it here? Get in touch and a human will help.
Getting started
Do I need any technical skills?
No. TAKEOVA is built for non-technical owners. If you can describe your business in plain English, you can build and run it — there's no code, and nothing to install.
How long does it take to get a site?
Minutes. TAKEOVA generates the design, copy, layout and structure from your description, then you refine from there at your own pace.
Can I change what TAKEOVA builds?
Yes — everything. Ask TAKEOVA in chat ("make the hero darker", "add a bookings page") or edit text, images and colours directly in the dashboard editor.
Your site & domain
Can I use my own domain?
Yes. Publish on your free TAKEOVA address to start, then connect a domain you already own whenever you're ready. If you don't have one yet, you can use the free address indefinitely.
Is hosting included?
Yes. Your published site is hosted for you — there's no separate hosting bill or server to manage.
Can I edit the site myself after it's built?
Absolutely. The dashboard editor lets you adjust copy, swap images, change colours and rearrange sections. Bigger changes are as easy as asking TAKEOVA in chat.
Billing
What does TAKEOVA cost?
You can start building for free. Paid plans and current pricing are listed on the pricing section of the home page. Billing is in Australian dollars (AUD).
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There's no lock-in — you can cancel from your account settings at any time. See the Terms & Refund policy for details on billing periods.
Do you offer refunds?
Refunds are handled under our published Refund & Cancellation policy — read it in full on the Legal page.
AI employees
What are AI employees?
They're built-in assistants that handle the ongoing busywork of running your business — things like answering enquiries, following up with customers, drafting content, preparing invoices and sending outreach — so going live isn't another pile of admin.
Do I have to use them?
No. They're there to help, but you stay in control — turn on the ones you want, review their work, and switch any of them off whenever you like.
Account & data
Is my data safe?
Your data is yours. We handle it under our Privacy policy, which covers how it's stored, who can access it, and our sub-processors. You can export or delete your data from your account.
How do I export or delete my account?
From account settings you can request an export of your data or delete your account. If you need a hand, contact us and we'll walk you through it.
Support
How do I get help from a person?
Head to the Contact page or email support@takeova.ai. We aim to reply within one business day.
