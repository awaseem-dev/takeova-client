Our story

Made by hand, made to matter

Ember & Oak began on a kitchen table with a single pot of wax and a stubborn belief that a candle should be honest — no shortcuts, no synthetic fillers, no waste.

Every candle is still poured by hand in small batches using 100% natural soy wax, cotton wicks, and phthalate-free fragrance. The glass is yours to keep, refill, and reuse.
