FAQ

Common questions

How is the winner chosen?

At the end of each month we rank all entered stores by daily average revenue — total revenue since the month started, divided by days you've been active. Top 5% win $500 cash automatically. No submission needed.

Is there really a $2,500 monthly ad-spend cap?

Yes — and it's strict. If your combined paid-ad spend across Meta, Google, and TikTok exceeds $2,500 in a calendar month, your store is excluded from that month's leaderboard. You stay on TAKEOVA and you're back at $0 the next month — but for that month you don't rank, even if your revenue is top 5%. The cap exists so the Showdown rewards businesses that AI Employees can transform, not businesses with the biggest ad budget.

How do you know how much I spent on ads?

After you enter, your TAKEOVA dashboard has a Showdown → Ad accounts section. Connect your Meta Business Manager, Google Ads, and TikTok Ads accounts (read-only) and we pull month-to-date spend automatically every day. You'll see a live $X / $2,500 counter and we warn you at 80% of cap. If you don't run paid ads on any platform, you self-attest at month end via a one-click form. Spot audits cover the honor-system path.

I joined mid-month. Am I at a disadvantage?

No — that's why we use daily average, not total. A store generating $500/day for 10 days ranks higher than a store generating $100/day for 30 days. Join any time and compete fairly.

I'm already a TAKEOVA customer. Does my existing revenue count?

Yes. Enter with your existing TAKEOVA account email and your revenue from the start of this month counts. Active stores are rewarded, not just new signups.

What revenue counts toward the competition?

Any order processed through TAKEOVA — store orders, bookings, invoices paid, course sales, and subscriptions. Revenue from your AI employees also counts — chatbot leads, Sales Rep follow-ups, Marketing Manager campaigns.

Is the $1M pool real?

Yes. 2,000 winners × $500 = $1,000,000. The pool runs across 6 monthly competitions — roughly 333 winners per month. The counter decrements publicly as winners are paid.

How does the $500 payout work?

If you win, you get an email with a claim link. Click it, connect your bank via Stripe (about 2 minutes), and we transfer $500 directly. Payouts process within 1 business day.

Can I enter more than once?

One entry per person per season. Once you're in, you automatically compete every month for the full 6 months — no need to re-enter.
